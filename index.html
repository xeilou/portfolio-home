Jensen Huang

Jensen Huang is a renowned technology entrepreneur and co-founder of NVIDIA, a leading company in the field of graphics processing units (GPUs) and artificial intelligence. With a background in electrical engineering and a visionary approach to computing, Huang has played a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology. He is known for his leadership in developing cutting-edge GPU technology that powers everything from video games to deep learning and autonomous vehicles. Jensen Huang's innovative contributions have made him a respected figure in the tech industry, and he continues to drive advancements in the world of graphics and AI computing.
